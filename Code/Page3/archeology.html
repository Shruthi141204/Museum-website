What is it?:
   Artifacts are objects or items that hold historical, cultural, or artistic significance. They are typically created or used by humans in the past and are considered valuable for their connection to a particular time period, civilization, or event. Artifacts can range from ancient tools, pottery, and weapons to religious artifacts, artwork, and even technological devices. They provide valuable insights into human history, helping us understand the past, explore different cultures, and preserve our collective heritage.
